Self Reflection

As I sit here in my room as I've done for a majority of my life,
I've sat and waited for things to happen to me.
It's hard to find the motivation when so many things you try fail to meet
your inner critic's judgement of where you should be at.
But just keep doing your best, trying with all your might until eventually,
you will get to where you want to be.
